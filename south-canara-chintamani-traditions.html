Our Family Traditions

The coastal rituals of South Canara blended with the Deccan warmth of Chintamani — a family living at the meeting point of two magnificent Karnataka cultures.

🌊
South Canara · Coastal Roots
Mangalore / Dakshina Kannada heritage — Tulu Nadu traditions including Nagaradhane (serpent worship), Bhuta Kola reverence, Yakshagana storytelling, Gowri-Ganesh Habba, patholi on turmeric leaves, and the deep matrilineal coastal spiritual identity. Language: Tulu / Kannada.
🌾
Chintamani · Deccan Upbringing
Chikballapur / Kolar plateau upbringing — Kannada-speaking town known for silk weaving and tomato farms. Vokkaliga-influenced cultural landscape, Ugadi celebrations with Obbattu, Karaga festival devotion, Dasara (Marnemi) traditions, and strong Kannada literary identity. Language: Kannada.
